Quality Of Brooms

Durability

A durable broom resists breakage and bristle loss, using strong materials like bamboo or synthetic fibers.

Stiffness/Flexibility

Stiff bristles suit rough surfaces, while softer ones protect delicate floors for efficient cleaning.

Material of Bristles

Natural fibers are eco-friendly and effective; synthetic bristles last longer and suit indoor use.

Ergonomic Handle

A well-designed handle ensures comfort, reduces strain, and enhances ease of use while sweeping.

Size of Broom Head

Larger heads clean big areas quickly; smaller heads reach tight spaces and corners efficiently.

Non-Slip Grip

A textured grip prevents slipping, ensuring safety and comfort during extended sweeping sessions.
